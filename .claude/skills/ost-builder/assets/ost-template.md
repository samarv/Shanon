## How to Use This Template

1. **Define your outcome** at the top - make it specific and measurable
2. **Map the experience** - identify 3-5 phases of the customer journey
3. **Populate opportunities** from customer interviews - organize by phase
4. **For target opportunities**, generate 3+ diverse solutions
5. **List assumptions** for each solution explicitly
6. **Design tests** for the riskiest assumptions
7. **Track active tests** in the table
8. **Update weekly** with learnings and new opportunities
9. **Archive** what's no longer relevant to keep it clean

## Quality Checks

Before sharing this tree, verify:
- [ ] Outcome is measurable with clear target
- [ ] Experience map reflects actual customer journey
- [ ] All opportunities are needs, not solutions
- [ ] Every opportunity has customer evidence
- [ ] Target opportunities have 3+ solutions
- [ ] Assumptions are explicitly listed
- [ ] Active tests are defined with success criteria
- [ ] Tree updated in last 7 days
