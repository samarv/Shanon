## Opening (5 minutes)

**Welcome script**:
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for taking the time to chat with me today. I'm learning about [context area], and I'd love to hear about your experience. This is just a conversation - there are no right or wrong answers. I'm not here to sell you anything or evaluate you in any way. I just want to understand how you work. Is it OK if I take notes as we talk?"

## Story Prompts (40-50 minutes)

### Story 1: [Topic Area 1]
**Opening question**: "Tell me about the last time you [did relevant task]."

**Follow-up prompts**:
- "What happened next?"
- "Tell me more about that."
- "How did that make you feel?"
- "What did you do then?"
- "Walk me through exactly what you did."

**Listen for**:
- Pain points and friction
- Workarounds and hacks
- Time sinks
- Confusion or uncertainty
- Emotional responses

### Story 2: [Topic Area 2]
**Opening question**: "Can you walk me through a recent time when you [different but related task]?"

**Follow-up prompts**:
- "What happened next?"
- "Tell me more about that."
- "How did you decide [decision they mentioned]?"
- "What made that difficult?"
- "How long did that take?"

### Story 3: [Topic Area 3]
**Opening question**: "Describe a recent situation where [edge case or struggle]."

**Follow-up prompts**:
- "What happened next?"
- "How did you handle that?"
- "What did you try first?"
- "What would have made that easier?"

## Closing (5 minutes)

**Wrap-up script**:
"This has been incredibly helpful, thank you so much for sharing your experience with me. Just to make sure I understood correctly, [summarize 1-2 key points]. Is that accurate?"

**Permission for follow-up**:
"Would it be OK if I reach out if I have follow-up questions?"

**Thank you**:
"I really appreciate your time today. The insights you've shared will be really valuable for [context]."
