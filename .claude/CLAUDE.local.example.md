## Quality Standards

### What "Good" Looks Like
- Starts with "Why?" not "What?"
- Explicit trade-offs stated
- Success metrics quantified
- Actionable next steps clear

- Generic advice without framework application
- Burying the ask at the end
- Summarizing without reading full content
